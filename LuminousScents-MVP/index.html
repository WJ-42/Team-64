How Luminous Scents works

This platform is designed as an educational fragrance shop. Users can explore perfumes, understand their composition, and place orders through a simple basket flow.

In later iterations the team will extend this with more detailed note filtering and a personalised recommendation quiz that uses previous choices and preferences.

  1. 1

    Browse fragrances

    Use the Fragrances page to see a curated list with price and key scent notes.

  2. 2

    Add to basket

    Add items you like. The basket stores your choices in the browser so you can review them.

  3. 3

    Review and checkout

    Open the Basket page to adjust quantities and view a running total for your order.

What this MVP delivers

Core E-commerce flow

Users can move from browsing to an active basket with quantity control and total calculation.

Basic account handling

The homepage form demonstrates input validation for registration and login using JavaScript.

Extensible design

The layout and navigation are ready to support future features such as recommendations and filters.
