Why Your Input Matters

At Luminous Scents, we believe that the finest fragrances are born from the desires and dreams of fragrance enthusiasts like you. Your suggestions help us understand what qualities matter most to our community.

Every product request is carefully reviewed by our master perfumers and product development team. While we cannot fulfill every request, your input directly influences our fragrance development roadmap.

What Happens Next?

  1. 1

    Review & Analysis

    Our team reviews your request within 5-7 business days, considering market trends and feasibility.

  2. 2

    Development Consideration

    Popular requests move to our development pipeline where our perfumers begin preliminary work.

  3. 3

    Notification & Updates

    If your request progresses, we'll notify you of developments and potential launch timelines.
