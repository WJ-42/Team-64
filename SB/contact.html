Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping Information

We offer free shipping on orders over £50. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days within the UK.

Returns Policy

30-day return policy for unopened items in original packaging. Contact us for return authorization.

Product Authenticity

All our fragrances are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized distributors.

Storage Tips

Store fragrances in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures to maintain quality.

Fragrance Concentrations

EDT (Eau de Toilette) lasts 3-5 hours, EDP (Eau de Parfum) lasts 5-8 hours, and Parfum lasts 8+ hours.

Choosing the Right Scent

Consider your personality, lifestyle, and when you'll wear it. Sample sets are available to help you decide.

Cruelty-Free Guarantee

All our products are cruelty-free. We never test on animals and work only with ethical suppliers.

Fragrance Longevity

Properly stored fragrances can last 3-5 years. Most lose quality after opening due to oxidation.

Sample Options

Try before you buy with our discovery sample sets and individual 2ml samples available for £3 each.

Payment Methods

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay for your convenience.

International Shipping

Yes! We ship worldwide. International delivery times vary by location, typically 7-14 business days.

Allergy Information

Each product page lists full ingredient lists. We recommend patch testing for sensitive skin.
